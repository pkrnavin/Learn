
## start glassfish
> cd <glassfish_home>/bin
> sudo ./asadmin start-domain

## stop glassfish
> cd <glassfish_home>/bin
> sudo ./asadmin stop-domain

## restart glassfish
> cd <glassfish_home>/bin
> sudo ./asadmin restart-domain


## deploy war file in linux
> cd <glassfish_home>/bin
> ./asadmin deploy --contextroot "appedo_velocityui" /home/fedora/20160511/appedo_velocityui.war
